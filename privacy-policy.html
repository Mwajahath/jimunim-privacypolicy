Privacy Policy for JIMUNIM

Last Updated: 04/Oct/2024

1. Information We Collect

JIMUNIM requires access to certain device permissions to provide its services effectively. Here's a breakdown of the information we collect and how we use it:

a. Contacts

Permissions: READ_CONTACTS, WRITE_CONTACTS
Usage: We request access to your contacts to help you import and manage business contacts, such as employees, customers, vendors, and sellers. This data remains on your device and is not shared or transmitted to our servers.

b. Camera

Permissions: CAMERA, android.hardware.camera, android.hardware.camera.autofocus
Usage: We use the camera to take photos, scan documents, or capture inventory images for business management. These photos are stored locally on your device or can be shared with external applications at your discretion. We do not store or transmit your photos to any external servers.

c. Storage

Permissions: READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE, WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE
Usage: We request access to your device's storage to allow you to upload images, access files, save data, and share files via external applications (e.g., WhatsApp) to support your business operations. This data is only stored on your device and is not shared with any third-party servers.

d. Internet Access

Permissions: INTERNET
Usage: We require internet access to enable certain features, such as syncing data, connecting with external services, or sharing files. We use encryption to protect your data during transmission.

e. SMS Permissions

Permissions: RECEIVE_SMS, READ_SMS, SEND_SMS
Usage: We use these permissions to automatically detect and fill in one-time passwords (OTPs) sent to your mobile number during login or registration for easier and faster account verification. We do not store or share your SMS data with any third parties, and it is used strictly for OTP capture.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide, improve, and maintain the services offered by JIMUNIM.
  • To import contacts for easy business management.
  • To capture photos for inventory, employee management, or documentation purposes.
  • To allow file storage and sharing for business operations.
  • To connect with external services (e.g., social media or messaging apps) through file sharing.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties except in the following situations:

  • With Your Consent: We will only share your information if you request or consent to sharing.
  • For Legal Purposes: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests from public authorities.
  • External Apps: Files shared using the App are handled directly by the external applications (e.g., WhatsApp). We do not store or monitor files shared through these third-party applications.

4. Data Security

We prioritize the security of your data. We implement industry-standard security practices to safeguard your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Your data is stored on your device, and we use encryption for data transmission over the internet.

5. Your Data Choices

You have control over your data and how it is used:

  • Permissions: You can manage the permissions granted to JIMUNIM at any time through your device settings. Please note that some features of the App may not function properly if certain permissions are denied.
  • Contact Import: The import of contacts is at your discretion, and the information remains on your device.
  • Photos and Files: You have control over which photos and files are shared or used within the App.

6. Children's Privacy

JIMUNIM is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices. We will notify users of significant changes by posting the new policy within the App and updating the "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

9. SMS Data Handling

We use SMS permissions to detect and autofill OTPs sent for account verification purposes only. No SMS data, apart from the OTP, is stored or transmitted. These permissions are requested solely to improve user experience by automating OTP entry during login or registration.

By using JIMUNIM, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this Privacy Policy.
