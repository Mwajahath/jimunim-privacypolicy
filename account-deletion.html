Request Account and Data Deletion

Your privacy is important to us. If you wish to request the deletion of your account and all associated data, please follow the instructions below:

How to Request Deletion

To delete your account and data, please send an email to our support team:

Email: support@jimunim.com

In your email, please include:

  • Your registered account email address
  • A statement requesting account and data deletion

What Happens Next?

Once we receive your request, we will process the deletion of your account and data within 30 days. You will receive a confirmation email once the process is completed.
